Augmented Reality: Understanding the Potential Benefits and Challenges

Reality Plus

Augmented reality, at its core, is simply taking a live view of the physical world and adding virtual elements to it.



Whether it's rendering what might be, or injecting something completely new, augmented reality promises to revolutionize our way of seeing and interacting with the world.

Augmented reality (abbreviated as AR) technologies take views of the real world (usually from a camera), and augment them with additional computer-generated sensory data. For instance, an AR smartphone app might display the view from the front camera and add virtual models to the real-world scene.
